sexualhealth.com

Health information source with online, confidential
medical testing with secure results delivery and consultation.

challenge

To merge an large, existing sexual health knowledge base web site with a proven online medical testing solution to provide customers a complete health care solution.

solution

Guiding users to the information and services they are looking for while preserving the first page search results ranking of the existing content were our highest priorities. Extensive research and exploration was done investigating the relationships of the information with the services to provide a rich experience.

the result

Sexualhealth.com established a beachhead on the internet as a complete source for sexual health education, testing and counseling.

my role

My primary role was lead user experience architect working closely with the Director of User Experience, Jon Kawa, the Chief Creative Officer, Michelle Sobel and designer, Jason Grant. I also worked on coding prototypes for our main website and mobile.
