western union payments legend

Mobile video game inspired by the 8-bit videos of the past.

challenge

Western Union tasked us to develop a digital solution that would build interest and drive potential customers to their tradeshow booths across the United States

solution

We developed Payments Legend — a mobile video game inspired by the days of 8-bit Nintendo games. While the users are trying to get the high score, they are learning about Western Union’s offerings and networking via LinkedIn integration with their fellow tradeshow visitors.

the result

With solid pre-tradeshow responses, traffic was increased to Western Union's booths where the game was presented on iPads. The high score leaderboard was displayed on a large flat panel screen with each player's LinkedIn photo, name and status.

my role

My role was digital strategist, ux architect, and javascript / rails developer. The screens were designed by Mike Mioduski at Pure.

application flow / api touchpoints

The incredibly small budget and fast turnaround required us to utilize several rapid-development solutions. An open source javascript-based pong game engine was customized and reskinned while being integrated with a Ruby on Rails backend.

linkedin integration

User's were encouraged to login with their LinkedIn profile for a more customized experience. LinkedIn was a more appropriate social source as the user base was focused on their profession and networking. This also allowed the user to leverage their LinkedIn status to promote their company on the leaderboard.

product messaging

Each level was started and finished with product messaging screens to educate the user in Western Union's product offering. By integrating these screens within the game play, we are able to avoid the application becoming too "sales-y."
