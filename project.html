sagwag

Raising donations with every turn of the crank.

challenge

Charity bike rides are a great way for an organization to raise money through individual efforts. The problem is donations end during the ride which is the most compelling part of the experience. The hitch was that we only had 3 weeks until the next ride.

solution

We created Sagwag to drive donors through social media to an online community where they could experience the ride in real time and make donations to help the rider when they really needed it. Using an integrated team of three made up of myself and two designers, we used tight collaboration and iterative process to crank out an alpha release within the tight timeframe.

technology

Built using Ruby on Rails for rapid development, we are leveraged the Facebook API for authentication and the broadcasting of signup and donation events. The Geoloci API was used for real-time geolocation of the rider and to fire events when the rider reached the toughest part of the ascent.

result

We tested Sagwag out as an alpha release during the Courage Classic, a three-day charity ride that benefits the Childrenâ€™s Hospital, with some success. The riders that activated their social networks were able to raise an additional 20% in pledges on the day of the event.
